Our Mission

Connecting children with professionals to inspire future career paths

Our Story

Evergreen Futures was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between children's career aspirations and real-world professional experiences. We believe that by connecting young minds with professionals from various fields, we can help them discover their passions and prepare for their future careers.

Professional Interviews

We conduct in-depth interviews with professionals from diverse industries, capturing their career journeys, challenges, and advice for young people.

Interactive Workshops

Our workshops bring professionals directly into classrooms and community centers, allowing children to engage with them in person.

College Preparation

We help students understand the connection between careers and education, guiding them toward appropriate college paths.

Our Approach

Direct Connection

We facilitate meaningful interactions between professionals and children, creating authentic learning experiences.

Career Exploration

We expose children to a wide range of career options, helping them discover paths they might not have considered.

Community Building

We create a supportive community where professionals can share their knowledge and children can learn from their experiences.
