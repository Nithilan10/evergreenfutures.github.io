Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with Evergreen Futures?

There are many ways to get involved! You can participate in our programs as a student, volunteer as a professional speaker or mentor, or support our mission through donations. Contact us to learn more about specific opportunities.

Are your programs free for students?

Yes, all our programs are completely free for students. We believe that career exploration should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial circumstances.

What age groups do you serve?

Our programs are designed for students in middle school through high school (ages 12-18). We also offer some opportunities for college students and young professionals.

How can professionals volunteer with your organization?

Professionals can volunteer as speakers, mentors, workshop leaders, or event support. We welcome volunteers from all industries and career backgrounds. Contact us to discuss specific opportunities.

Do you offer programs outside of the Bay Area?

Currently, our in-person programs are primarily in the Bay Area, but we're expanding! We also offer some virtual programs that are accessible nationwide. Contact us to learn about programs in your area.

How can I stay updated about new programs and events?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or check our blog regularly for updates about new programs, events, and opportunities to get involved.
